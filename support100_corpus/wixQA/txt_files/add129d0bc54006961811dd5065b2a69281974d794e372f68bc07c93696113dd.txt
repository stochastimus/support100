Changing a Domain After Purchase (Correcting a Typo)
It is not possible to edit or modify a domain once purchased. When you register a domain name you reserve the letters or characters in that order. If you have already made the purchase, the only way to change the domain name is to register a new domain.Important:Domains purchased through Wix are set to renew automatically. We recommend that you turn the auto renewal option off for the incorrect domain to avoid paying for another year.
