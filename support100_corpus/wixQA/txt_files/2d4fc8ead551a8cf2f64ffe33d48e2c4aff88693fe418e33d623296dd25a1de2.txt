Wix Blog: Deleting Your Blog
You may have selected a template that includes a blog, or you simply don't need it anymore. Whatever the reason, you can delete your blog from your site at any time.Tip:Deleting Wix Blog does not permanently delete your content. Re-adding it restores your content, but it does not restore your settings. It also does not re-add any additional blog elements, e.g. Post List etc.Wix EditorStudio Editor
Click Pages & Menu  on the left side of the editor. Hover Blog Pages and click the Settings  icon. Click Delete Blog. Click Delete to confirm.Note:If you've set your blog as your homepage, you need to delete the entire homepage. If you want to delete it, you can set a different page as the homepage and copy all the elements you had in your previous homepage. Learn more.
Click the Pages  icon on the left side of the editor. Scroll down to Blog Pages. Hover over Blog Pages and click the More actions   icon. Click Delete Blog.  Click Delete to confirm.Note:If you've set your blog as your homepage, you need to delete the entire homepage.
