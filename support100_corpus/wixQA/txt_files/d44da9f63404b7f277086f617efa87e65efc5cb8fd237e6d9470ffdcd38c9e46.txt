Wix Bookings: Upgrading Your Site
Allow clients to start booking your services by upgrading your site. Alternatively, you can display your services (without allowing bookings) on a free Wix site.Wix offers a range of site plans especially designed for businesses. To start getting booked and accepting payments, visit our Upgrade page to find a plan that best suits your business needs. To choose a plan:
If you have not already done so, add Wix Bookings to your site. Go to your site's dashboard. Click Upgrade.
 Click Select under the plan that suits your needs. Click to select the relevant subscription period and click Continue to Checkout. Enter your payment details. Click Submit Purchase.Important:
After submitting your purchase, your bank may require you to verify your identity. If you're experiencing any issues with the authentication process, contact your bank directly. If you've previously purchased a site plan that doesn't support Wix Bookings, you can upgrade to a plan that does. When switching plans, if the new plan costs more than the original plan you purchased, you pay only the difference in price.
