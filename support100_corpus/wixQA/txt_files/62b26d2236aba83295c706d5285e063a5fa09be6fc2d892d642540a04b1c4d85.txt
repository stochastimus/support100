Wix Payments: Receiving Your First Payout
Every payment you accept through Wix Payments must be processed and scheduled before we can send it to your bank account.Your first payout can only be scheduled once your Wix Payments account is successfully verified. After receiving your first payment, it's available for payout within an average of 7 days.You can see when your payout is scheduled to be sent directly from the Payouts area in your Wix Payments dashboard. Once sent, it can take between 3-5 business days for the payout to reach your bank account.For future payouts, you have the option to select a schedule (daily, weekly, monthly) that fits your needs.Notes:
