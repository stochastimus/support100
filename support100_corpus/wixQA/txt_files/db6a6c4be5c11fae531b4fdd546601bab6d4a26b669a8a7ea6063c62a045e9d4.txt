Google Workspace: Connecting a Business Email for a Domain Pointing to Wix
If you connected your domain to Wix via pointing and you purchased a Google Workspace business email, you must add the Google Workspace MX records in your domain host account (not Wix) to connect your business email. Important:Instructions for adding MX records vary between domain hosts. Contact your domain host if you need help adding the Google Workspace MX records. To connect Google Workspace for a domain connected via pointing:Log in to your domain host account where your DNS records are managed. Add the following MX records to your DNS settings:Tip:View Google's list of host-specific directions for adding Google Workspace MX records. Name/Host/AliasTime to Live (TTL*)Record TypePriorityValue/Answer/DestinationBlank or @3600MX1ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM.Blank or @3600MX5ALT1.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM.Blank or @3600MX5ALT2.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM.Blank or @3600MX10ALT3.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM.Blank or @3600MX10ALT4.ASPMX.L.GOOGLE.COM.Note about TTLOnce the MX records are set up correctly, Google recommends changing the TTL value to 86400. For more information from Google about Google Workspace MX records click here.
