Wix Payments: Guidelines for Uploading Documentation
Wix may request that you send the following documents in order to verify your Wix Payments account. The photo ID is used to verify that the identity of the Wix Payments account owner (personal or business) matches the identity of the bank account owner.Important:All documents* must be issued within the same country you select as your business location. Documents may be rejected if issued from another country.(*For a business Wix Payments account, your ID may be issued from any Wix Payments supported country).Learn about the guidelines for uploading documents for:
Photo identification Address verification  Bank details Company detailsPhoto IDYour photo ID must meet the following requirements:
Your photo ID can be a passport, driver's license or a government issued ID card Your photo ID must be active and non-expired The photo must be in color Your ID must show all 4 borders and corners The ID must not have information blocked or blacked out The ID must be straightened, not angled or rotated The ID must not be blurry, overexposed or copied from another source (e.g. screenshot) Your first, middle and last name in your Wix Payments account must match those of your ID Your birth date in your Wix Payments account must match that of your ID For passports with a Machine-Readable Zone (MRZ), the MRZ must be visible. For driver's licenses or government issue ID cards, send a separate file for each side Documents must be sent in one of the following: HEIC, JPEG, JPG, PDF, PNG The maximum file size is 10 MB The minimum file size is 1 KB for PDF, 100 KB for other formatsAddress verification documentProvide a document, such as utility bill, which verifies the address you have submitted. Documents must be visible and clear Documents must be dated within the last six months Complete documents must be uploaded. A complete document is defined as:

A bank statement A voided check Official letters issued by a bankSee below for the requirements for uploading each of these types of documents.Bank statement
Provide a color copy of your statement The bank logo should be visible and clear The full bank account number should be visible and clear  The document must have been issued within the last 12 months The bank account holder’s name should be visible and clear and match the owner of the Wix Payments account for a personal Wix Payments account The name of the business checking account needs to be visible and clear and match the legal name of the business listed on the company document, and also the legal company name in your Wix Payments dashboard for a business Wix Payments accountVoided check
The word "VOID" should appear on the check The bank's logo should be visible and clear The bank account number should be visible and clear Bank account holder/company name must be printed, not handwritten The check must be issued and printed by the bank Voided check must not be part of a direct deposit form The bank account holder’s name should be visible and clear and match the owner of the Wix Payments account for a personal Wix Payments account The name of the business checking account needs to be visible and clear and match the legal name of the business listed on the company document, and also the legal company name in your Wix Payments dashboard for a business Wix Payments accountBank letter
The bank logo should be visible and clear The bank account number should be visible and clear The letter must be issued and printed by the bank The document must have been issued within the last 12 months The letter must have a bank agent signature The bank account holder’s name should be visible and clear and match the owner of the Wix Payments account for a personal Wix Payments account The name of the business checking account needs to be visible and clear and match the legal name of the business listed on the company document, and also the legal company name in your Wix Payments dashboard for a business Wix Payments accountGuidelines for uploading bank statements, voided checks and bank lettersBanking documents may be rejected if:

You may have changed your business name and the name on the business registration document does not match your current legal business name. If so, please upload the business registration document you currently have to your Wix Payments account. In addition, you may be requested to provide an official name change documentation that displays both the original and new legal business name. If you need assistance, please contact Customer Care. If you have recently married, but your ID does not reflect your new surname, you'll need to provide a copy of your marriage certificate. You can submit it by uploading a PDF of your marriage cert with your Photo ID. The marriage certificate can be added as the second page to a PDF file along with the ID. If you need assistance, please contact Customer Care.
