Wix Stores: Limits & Recommended Guidelines
To ensure that the Wix Stores platform remains stable and that your online store is usable and fast, Wix has set some limitations.Wix Stores platform limits:
Products: You can upload up to 50,000 products from the Wix Stores Dashboard manually or by CSV import. Product Options per Product: Each product can have up to 6 product options (e.g. color, size, material). Choices per Product Option: Each product option (e.g. size) can have up to 100 option choices (e.g. Green, Blue) Product Variants: You can create up to 1,000 variants per product.What happens if you exceed any of these limits?When you approach or exceed a limit, you'll first receive a notification in the Wix Stores Dashboard. When you exceed one of these limits, you’ll receive an error message.
