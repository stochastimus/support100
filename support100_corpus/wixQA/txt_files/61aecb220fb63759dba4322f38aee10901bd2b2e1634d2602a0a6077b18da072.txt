Changing the Title Displayed in the Browser Tab
By default, your page's title tag is displayed as the title in your browser tab. If you want to change the browser tab title, you can customize your page's title tag in the SEO Panel of your page.In this article, learn how to:
Change the browser tab title for your live site Change the Editor browser tab titleChanging the browser tab title for your live siteYou can edit your browser tab title of your live site at any time in your site's Editor. Search engines will update your page's title tag the next time that they crawl your site. If you want to try to speed up the process, you can submit your page's URL to search engines.To change the browser tab title for your live site:Wix EditorStudio Editor
Go to your editor. Click Pages & Menu  on the left side of the editor. Click the More Actions icon  next to the relevant page. Click SEO basics. Enter the page title under Title tag. Publish your site.

Try removing any custom code you added to your site if you see an incorrect browser tab title.Changing the browser tab title for your editorYou can also change the browser title of the editor version of your site. This helps you quickly identify the relevant site in your browser bar, especially if you are working on multiple Wix sites.These steps are only possible if you haven't connected a domain to your site. If you have a domain, you must unassign the domain first and then reassign the domain when finished. To change the browser tab title of your site editor:
(Optional) Unassign your domain. Go to site's dashboard. Click Site Actions. Click Rename Site. Under Site Address (URL), enter the new browser title. (Optional) Reassign your domain.
