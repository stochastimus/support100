Setting up Your Payment Method in Wix Hotels
Important:This article refers to the original Wix Hotels. If you are using the new Wix Hotels by HotelRunner, which is not yet available to all users, please read the information here. Learn how to check which version of Wix Hotels you are using. To start renting out rooms and collecting income, set up payment methods. You can also choose a hotel currency, set a tax rate and set deposits.To set up a payment method:Go to your dashboard.Click the Hotels tab.Click the Payments tab.Select a Payment Method and enter your details: Paypal: Get paid via one of the most popular payment gateways available on the web. Paypal supports registration from almost all countries.Stripe: Quick setup and you're set to get online bookings. Supports registration from 14 countries and more than 100 currencies.Mercado Pago: This payment is for guests from Argentina, Brazil, Chile, Colombia, Mexico and Venezuela. Guests are asked for their ID number when booking.Offline Payments: Instantly get bookings from the web without a connection to a payment gateway. Guests aren't charged when they book online. They can pay in person.Enter your Price Settings: Currency: Set the currency for your hotel. Your room prices are displayed in this currency.Tax: Set the tax rate for your hotel (if needed).Deposit: Set the amount you want to receive upfront when guests book online.  Note: This is only available for online payments.Click Save.Note:You can set up and accept multiple payment methods in Wix Hotels.
