Email Marketing: Upgrading Your Email Marketing Plan
To get access to more email marketing features upgrade your email marketing plan and choose the package that works for your business.To upgrade your email marketing plan:
Go to Email Marketing in your site's dashboard. Under Monthly Balance click Upgrade. Choose a plan that works best for you and checkout.Want to compare plans before upgrading?Compare the different email marketing plans to see which is right for your business. Choose your plan by the number of emails you’re planning to send a month and any additional features you are interested in.
