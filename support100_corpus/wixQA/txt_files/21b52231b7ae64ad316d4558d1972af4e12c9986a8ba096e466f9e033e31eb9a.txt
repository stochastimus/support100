Site Members: Deleting Your Members Area
Want to remove the Members Area from your site? You can do it in a few quick steps directly from your editor. Deleting this area removes all member pages, including the default ones that were added automatically (e.g. My Account, My Orders) and any pages you've created on your own. In this article, learn more about:
