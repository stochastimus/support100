Transferring a .co.uk Domain Away from Wix
Wix domains with a .co.uk suffix require a change of the IPS tag name in order to transfer them away from Wix. This process does not involve an authorization code (EPP code) like with other domain transfers.Before you begin:You need an IPS tag to submit a transfer request for .co.uk domains. Contact your new domain provider to get the IPS tag before starting the transfer process.To transfer your domain away from Wix:
Go to Domains in your Wix account. Click the Domain Actions icon  next to the relevant domain. Click Transfer away from Wix. Click Transfer Domain after reviewing the important details of the process. Enter your new provider's IPS tag and click Continue. Click Submit Request to confirm your transfer.What's next?You don't need to do anything else in your Wix account. Your new domain provider can provide the next steps. Once the process is complete, the domain transfer will be finalized within 7 days.
